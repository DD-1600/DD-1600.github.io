FAQ

How do I get started?

Tap Begin climbing on the home screen. The 25-minute focus session starts, the climber begins walking up the active peak, and a Live Activity appears on the Lock Screen and Dynamic Island. When the session completes, the App moves you to the break screen for 5 minutes.

Does my progress sync between my iPhone and Apple Watch?

Yes — start, pause, and reset controls are mirrored on the paired Apple Watch app, and your session history syncs across your own devices via iCloud Key-Value storage. There is no separate account; everything is keyed to your Apple ID.

Why does the app ask for HealthKit access?

When you grant access, Peak Pomodoro writes each completed focus session as a Mindful Minutes entry in Apple Health, and reads your existing Mindful Minutes to show how your sessions add up over time. HealthKit data never leaves your device by way of Peak Pomodoro. Granting is optional — the app continues to function if you decline.

How do peaks unlock?

Mt. Whitney is available from the first session. Denali unlocks after 10 completed sessions, Kilimanjaro after 50, Everest after 150. You can switch between unlocked peaks any time from the Settings popover.

How do I leave a tip?

Open Settings → ABOUT → Support the climb. Three tiers are available — Base Camp, Summit, and Everest — purely as a thank-you. Tips do not unlock features; every feature in Peak Pomodoro is free for everyone forever.

How do I restore my tips on a new device?

Open Settings → ABOUT → Support the climb and tap Restore Purchases. StoreKit will re-attach any tips you’ve previously left under the same Apple ID.

How do I delete my data?

Uninstall the app. All data is stored on-device. HealthKit data can also be cleared in Settings → Privacy & Security → Health → Peak Pomodoro → Delete All Data. iCloud Key-Value entries can be cleared by signing out of iCloud or resetting iCloud preferences.

How do I report a bug?

Email danieldavis16@gmail.com with:

  • Your iOS version (Settings → General → About → iOS Version)
  • Your device model
  • Steps to reproduce
  • A screenshot if relevant

How do I request a feature?

Same email — we read every message.

Is there a Mac, iPad, or visionOS version?

Not yet. The current build is iPhone-only with a paired Apple Watch app. iPad and visionOS are on the roadmap.
