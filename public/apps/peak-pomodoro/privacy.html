Peak Pomodoro

Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-05-11

Peak Pomodoro (“the App”) is published by Northland Studios (“we”, “us”). This policy explains what data the App collects, how it’s used, and your choices.

Summary

  • We do not sell your data.
  • We do not track you across other apps or websites.
  • All app data stays on your device or in your own iCloud account.

What we collect

The categories below align with Apple’s App Privacy nutrition-label format. Only the categories that actually apply to Peak Pomodoro appear here — anything not listed is not collected.

  • Health & Fitness: if you grant HealthKit access, the App writes each completed focus session as a Mindful Minutes entry, and reads your existing Mindful Minutes to show how your sessions add up over time. HealthKit data is stored on-device by Apple and is never transmitted off your device by Peak Pomodoro.
  • User Content (on-device only): session history, peak progression, streak counts, and preferences. Stored on your device via Apple’s standard preferences storage, and optionally synced to your iCloud account via iCloud Key-Value storage so your stats follow you across your own iPhone and Apple Watch. We do not have access to this data.
  • Purchases: if you choose to leave a tip via the in-app tip jar, the purchase is handled entirely by Apple. We see anonymized receipt data from StoreKit only — enough to verify a purchase, nothing else.
  • Identifiers: none. We do not use IDFA or any cross-app identifier. We do not link any data to a user identity.
  • Diagnostics: crash logs and performance data only if you opted in to Share With App Developers in iOS Settings. Aggregated by Apple, no personal info.

How we use it

HealthKit data is used only to display your Mindful Minutes inside Peak Pomodoro and to record completed focus sessions in Apple Health. Session history and preferences are used to drive the timer, the peak progression, and the stats screen. Purchase receipts are used only to verify tips and to surface the optional supporter badge. We do not use any of this data for advertising or third-party analytics.

Data sharing

The App does not share data with third parties, except:

  • Apple, for the purposes you would expect: App Store delivery, opt-in crash reports, in-app purchase processing, HealthKit storage (Apple-managed, encrypted on-device), iCloud Key-Value storage of your own preferences and stats.

We will only disclose data if required by law (e.g. a valid subpoena) and will notify you where legally permitted.

Data retention

Data stored on your device stays on your device until you delete the App or clear it from Apple Health. Data synced to iCloud Key-Value storage stays in your own iCloud account; deleting the App removes the local copy but Apple retains the iCloud key-value entry per your iCloud settings. We do not maintain server-side copies.

Your rights

  • Delete all App data by uninstalling the App. For HealthKit data: Settings → Privacy & Security → Health → Peak Pomodoro → Delete All Data.
  • Contact us with privacy questions at danieldavis16@gmail.com.

If you’re in the EU, UK, or California, you have additional rights under GDPR / UK GDPR / CCPA — contact us and we’ll honor them. Because we don’t transmit data off-device, most of these rights resolve to “delete the App.”

Children

The App is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from them. If you believe a child has provided information to us, contact danieldavis16@gmail.com and we’ll address it.

Changes

We’ll update this page if data practices change. The “Effective date” at the top reflects the current version. Material changes will also be flagged in the App’s release notes.
